About Me

Hi! I'm someone who loves calm melodies and curious questions. My favourite colour is light blue — like a clear sky just before evening. When I'm not making music, I'm reading about how the universe works and experimenting with cool science concepts. I believe that creativity and curiosity are the best tools for understanding the world.
